Health Programs

**XIII. Course Fee Courtesy**

The [Course Fee Courtesy
Policy](/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/faculty-handbook/course_fee_courtesy.html "Course fee courtesy ") applies
to full‐time Health Programs faculty upon the commencement of the first
reappointment. The costs of the course fee courtesy program for HP
faculty members will be paid by the relevant College. See
the Faculty Handbook for program information.*\
