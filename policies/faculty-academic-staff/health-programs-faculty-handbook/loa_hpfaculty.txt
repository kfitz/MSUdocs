Health Programs

**XII. Leaves of Absence**

The following leaves that are described in
the [MSU *Faculty* *Handbook* ](/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/faculty-handbook/index.html "faculty handbook")apply
to Health Programs faculty: 

-   Bereavement
-   Family and Medical Leave
-   Leave of Absence Without Pay
-   Military
-   Parental
-   Short-Term Disability (medical)
-   Vacation
