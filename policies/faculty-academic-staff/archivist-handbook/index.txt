Introduction - Archivist Continuous Appointment System Handbook {#introduction---archivist-continuous-appointment-system-handbook xmlns=""}
===============================================================

This document contains employment policies specifically related to
employment as an Archivist in the continuous appointment system in the
University Archives & Historical Collections (UAHC). This document does
not replace or modify other existing University policies, which remain
in effect for all University employees. In circumstances when this
document is silent on an issue, the UAHC adheres to the corresponding
policy contained in the Faculty Handbook.

This handbook is located on the archives' shared drive at:
S:\\Administrative\\Policies and Procedures\\UAHC Staff
Handbook\\Archivist Handbook.docx
