**1.6 Non-Reappointment**

An archivist who is not to be recommended for reappointment by the
Director of the University Archives must be notified in writing by the
Director by the December 15 preceding the expiration of his/her
probationary appointment. Copies of the notification are to be sent to
the Provost. Upon written request of the archivist, the Director shall
transmit in writing the reasons for not recommending further
appointment.\
