**3.1. Academic Governance**

All archivists in the Michigan State University Archives & Historical
Collections may actively participate in matters concerning the academic
governance of the UAHC. Such participation is recognized as an important
and integral part of the individual archivist\'s responsibilities and as
an element in continuing professional development.\
