**2.2.3. Criterion III -- Service and/or Professional Activities**

The purpose of this criterion is to evaluate the archivist's impact on
the UAHC and Michigan State University, the community, and the
profession and the probability that positive impact and sustained growth
will continue in the future. No priority of the elements included is
intended.

-   Continuing active involvement in administration of professional
    organizations relevant to archives or records management
-   Service on local, state, regional, national or international
    archival committees
-   Service on university-wide committees or councils
-   Community service and volunteer work
-   Serving as an academic or community advisor
-   Serving as a mentor in a mentoring relationship
-   Any other pursuits which demonstrate the archivist\'s impact on the
    UAHC, Michigan State University, the community, or the profession
