Academic Hiring Manual

**University Approved Academic Titles**

::: {xmlns=""}
Also see [University Approved Academic
Position/Ranks](/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/academic-hiring-manual/academic_positions_ranks.html).
:::

Emeritus or acting designations can be used with any title when
appropriate.

EEO code identifies a level of administrative responsibility which is
used for federal reporting purposes.

10 = executive management level administrative positions, \
20 = the next administrative level down, i.e. management \
30 = positions which are not considered administrative for reporting
purposes.\
