Academic Hiring Manual

**1.4- Use of \"Dean\" and \"Chairperson\" in Academic Hiring Manual**

\"Dean\" is used to denote the head of a Major Administrative Unit (MAU)
and refers to MAU heads with titles of Dean, Director, Vice President,
etc. \"Chairperson\" is used to denote the head of a department-level
unit and refers to department-level unit heads with titles of
Chairperson, School Director or Director.\
