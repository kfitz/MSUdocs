**5.4 Paying an Individual on an On-Call Appointment**

Access the MSU CATs system via the EBS portal to submit payments for
on-call work.\
\
Payments cannot be entered in CATS until an Appointment or Reappointment
covering the pay period has been entered into the HR/Payroll System and
the Contract and Grant Administration Office has approved any contract
or grant accounts.\
\
