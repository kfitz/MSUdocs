**5.3 Reappointing an On-Call Individual**

Fill out a Reappointment/Rehire form. There are no attachments for U.S.
citizens with the exception of the [Form I-9 Employment Eligibility
Verification](https://hr.msu.edu/ua/i9/index.html "I9 Information") if
the employee was previously exempt from completing an I-9 or if there
has been a break in paid service of one year or greater; send the
completed [Form I-9 Employment Eligibility
Verification](https://hr.msu.edu/ua/i9/index.html "I9 Information") if
the individual is a foreign national to Human Resources, Suite 110
Nisbet Building, East Lansing, MI 48823.

The Reappointment/Rehire form is routed in the same manner as described
in [Processing an On-Call
Appointment](/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/academic-hiring-manual/processing_on-call_appointment.html).\
