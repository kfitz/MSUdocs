Academic Hiring Manual

**6.1 Final Approval of Academic Actions**

::: {xmlns=""}
**Actions approved by the Board of Trustees**
:::

::: {xmlns=""}
-   Appointment of tenure system faculty with tenure
-   Appointments of deans, head coaches of football, men\'s basketball
    and men\'s hockey and certain executive management and academic
    administrative staff
-   Promotion of tenure system faculty with award of tenure
-   Reappointment of tenure system faculty with award of tenure
-   Extension of contract for head coaches of football, men\'s
    basketball and men\'s hockey
-   Actions approved by the President
-   Change of Status for Head Coaches of football, men\'s basketball and
    men\'s hockey
-   Emeritus designation of executive management and other appointees
    whose administrative appointment requires approval by the Board
:::

**Actions approved by Vice Presidents**

::: {xmlns=""}
-   All actions for certain executive management staff
:::

**Actions approved by the Office of the Provost**

::: {xmlns=""}
-   Appointment of tenure system faculty without tenure
-   Appointment of certain executive management staff
-   Appointment of librarians with award of continuing employment
-   Appointment of librarians in the continuing system without award of
    continuing employment
-   Appointment of specialists, MSU Extension staff, FRIB/NSCL staff,
    and those with no rank, in a continuing system, with or without the
    award of continuing employment
-   Promotion of tenure system faculty without the award of tenure
-   Promotion of librarians with the award of continuing employment
-   Promotion of librarians without the award of continuing employment
-   Reappointment of tenure system faculty without award of tenure
-   Reappointment of librarians with or without the award of continuing
    employment
-   Change of Status without an end date for tenure system faculty
-   Change of Status without an end date for deans and executive
    management
-   Changes of Status without an end date for those in continuing
    systems except tenure system faculty, and executive management staff
-   Leaves of Absence for those in continuing systems
-   Terminations/Cancellations of appointments in continuing systems and
    for executive management staff and head coaches
:::

**Additional Actions approved by the Office of the Provost**

::: {xmlns=""}
-   Appointment of fixed term faculty and academic staff, excluding
    executive management and head coaches
-   Promotion of FRIB/NSCL Staff with or without the award of continuing
    employment
-   Reappointment of specialists, MSU Extension, and FRIB/NSCL staff
    with or without the award of continuing employment
-   Change of Status with an end date, excluding those for head coaches
-   Change of Status to change terminal leave/retirement date(s)
-   Leaves of Absence for those on fixed term appointments, excluding
    executive management
-   Terminations/Cancellations of fixed term appointments, excluding
    head coaches
