Continuing Appointment System of FRIB-NSCL

**Appendix IX: Administrative Structure of the FRIB/NSCL**

While this document refers only to department heads and the director, in
all cases the FRIB/NSCL organizational structure shall be followed. That
is appointment recommendations, grievances and other matters should be
routed through the FRIB/NSCL structure currently in effect.\
