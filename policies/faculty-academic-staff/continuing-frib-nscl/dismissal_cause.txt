Continuing Appointment System of FRIB-NSCL

**10. Dismissal for Cause**

**10.1.** A staff member in the CA System, whether on probationary
appointment or Continuing Status, may be dismissed for cause. When such
an action is contemplated, the formal procedures described in [Appendix
VI](https://www.hr.msu.edu/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/continuing-frib-nscl/appendixVI_dismissal.html)
will be followed.\
