Academic Specialist Manual

**6.5 Resignation**

::: {xmlns=""}
Academic specialists in the Academic Specialist Appointment System who
plan to resign must give at least 90 days\' notice in writing to the
unit administrator. Unique or unusual situations which may result in
fewer than 90 days\' notice for resignation need to be discussed with
the unit administrator with notification date determined by mutual
agreement.\
