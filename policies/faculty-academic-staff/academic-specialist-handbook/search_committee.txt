Academic Specialist Manual

**2.6 Search Committees for Specified University-Level Administrators**

Academic specialists are eligible to participate in the selection of
specified University level administrators as provided in the
\"Procedures for Faculty and Student Participation in the  Selection of
Specified University Level Administrators,\" as contained in the Faculty
Handbook.  This involvement consists of membership on advisory
committees to the President and/or the Provost that assist in the
selection of the listed administrators.\
