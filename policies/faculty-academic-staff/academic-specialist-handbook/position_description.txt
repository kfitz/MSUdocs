Academic Specialist Manual

**4.1 Position Description**

A copy of the  [Specialist Position
Description](https://hr.msu.edu/ua/forms/faculty-academic-staff/info-spd.html "Info about Specialist Position Description Form")
 form, specific criteria for promotion, a copy of the unit evaluation
procedures used for an academic specialist appointed in the Academic
Specialist Appointment System, and a copy of the  [Faculty Grievance
Policy](/policies-procedures/faculty-academic-staff/faculty-handbook/grievance_policy.html "Faculty Grievance Policy")
 shall be provided to the academic specialist upon initial appointment,
together with a copy of the letter of offer, a summary of benefits and,
upon request, a copy of the \" [Fixed Term Faculty/Academic Staff
Appointment/Reappointment
Memorandum](https://hr.msu.edu/ua/forms/faculty-academic-staff/info-fixed-term-memorandum.html "Fixed Term Appointments")
.\" Copies of the position description and evaluation procedures must be
sent to the Office of Academic Human Resources along with the
appointment form. These materials will be placed in the academic
specialist\'s file in the Office of Academic Human Resources.\
