Academic Specialist Manual

**3.5 Minimum Salaries**

The Office of the Provost, in consultation with the Academic Specialist
Advisory Committee to the Associate Provost and Associate Vice President
for Academic Human Resources and the University Committee on Faculty
Affairs, will establish and maintain a set of  minimum salaries  for
academic specialist appointments.\
