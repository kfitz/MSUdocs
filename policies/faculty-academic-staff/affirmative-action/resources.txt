Affirmative Action

**11. Affirmative Action Resources - Affirmative Action Handbook**

::: {xmlns=""}
The following organizations, committees and offices undertake a variety
of activities which promote the goals and the spirit of affirmative
action. They constitute a valuable resource on campus. Representatives
from these groups may provide assistance in your efforts to enlarge the
pool of candidates, to help you answer questions raised by candidates
and to assist in your recruitment efforts by meeting with candidates or
writing to them describing their activities.
:::
