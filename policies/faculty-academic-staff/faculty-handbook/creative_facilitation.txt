**Facilitation of Research and Creative Efforts**

::: {xmlns=""}
**VI. RESEARCH AND CREATIVE ENDEAVOR (*Cont.*)**
:::

Departmental and college facilities and resources are available in most
units to partially support research and creative efforts of the faculty.
In addition, the University maintains an array of facilities and
programs which can be drawn upon for support. These include
the [Library](http://www.lib.msu.edu/ "Library"), the Office of the Vice
President for Research and Graduate Studies, various divisions such as
the [Computer Laboratory](http://ats.msu.edu/ "Computer Lab"), [Office
of Radiation, Chemical and Biological
Safety](http://www.orcbs.msu.edu/ "ORCBS info"), [University Laboratory
Animal
Resources](http://www.msu.edu/unit/ular/ "University Laboratory Animal Resources"), [Instructional
Media
Center](http://imc.msu.edu/ "Instructional Media Center"), [Broadcasting
Services](http://www.wkar.msu.edu/ "Broadcasting Services"), etc. and
various centers and institutes for facilitating multi and
interdisciplinary research projects. Solicitation of funds for research
by the faculty is encouraged within established policies and
procedures.\
