**Code of Teaching Responsibility**

 Last updated: 3/27/2012

**V. INSTRUCTION *(Cont.)***

and the Academic Senate on November 19, 1969; it was subsequently
revised by Academic Council on May 19, 1976, February 27, 1996, and
April 19, 2005 (effective Fall semester 2005*); it was revised by
University Council on March 27, 2012* *.   **

The Code of Teaching Responsibility is available in the  [Academic

 catalog on the Office of the Registrar website.\
