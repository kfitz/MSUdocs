**Student Employee Responsibilities**

::: {xmlns=""}
The work performed by student employees contributes to the success of
their departments and the university. As Michigan State
University employees, students have certain work-related
responsibilities:
:::

::: {xmlns=""}
-   Student employees must verify their social security card name and
    number, and complete all required payroll forms, along with the I-9
    document, accurately and in a timely manner.
-   Student employees must adhere to the rules, policies and procedures
    of Michigan State University and the hiring department.
-   Student employees must respect the confidentiality of all
    university, department and student documents and information.
-   Student employees have a responsibility to learn their jobs and
    perform them to the best of their ability.
-   Student employees have a responsibility to treat others in the
    workplace with fairness and respect.
-   Student employees have a responsibility to inform their employers of
    changes in their class schedules or enrollment status that would
    affect their student employment jobs.
