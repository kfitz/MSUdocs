**Seeking Information on Sexual Harassment**\
A member of the University community who seeks information regarding
sexual harassment and this Policy may contact:

[Women\'s Resource
Center](http://wrc.msu.edu/ "Women's Resource Center")\
[Office of Inclusion and Intercultural
Initiatives](http://www.inclusion.msu.edu/ "Office for Inclusion and Intercultural Initiatives")\
The administrator in charge (e.g. chairperson, directory, dean, vice
president) of the relevant academic or support unit\
[Grievance
Official](https://www.msu.edu/~fgo/ "Faculty Grievance Office")\
[Employee Assistance
Program](http://eap.msu.edu/ "Employee Assistance Program")\
[University Undergraduate
Division](https://www.msu.edu/dept/uud/main.html "Undergraduate University Division")\
[Ombudsperson](https://www.msu.edu/unit/ombud/ "Office of the Ombudsperson")\
[Director of Student
Life](http://studentlife.msu.edu/ "Department of Student Life")\
[Director of MSU Student
Employment](/policies-procedures/student-handbook/index.html "Contact Student Employment ")

**As a supervisor, you should:**

::: {xmlns=""}
-   know MSU\'s policy on sexual harassment and recommended procedures
    for handling complaints of sexual harassment;
-   know EEOC and state regulations which pertain to sexual harassment -
    consult with the Ombudsperson, the Director of the Division of
    Women\'s Programs if necessary;
:::

In processing sexual harassment complaints, the responsible
administrator will normally confer with the Office of the General
Counsel and academic or human resources administrators. Guidelines for
investigating allegations of sexual harassment are available to
administrators.
