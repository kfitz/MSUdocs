**Voluntary Benefits**

 **VII. FACULTY AND ACADEMIC STAFF BENEFIT AND RETIREMENT PROGRAMS
(*Cont.*)**\
\
Voluntary benefits include long term care, vision, pre-paid legal, pet
insurance, critical illness, home-auto insurance. These programs may
have open enrollment periods that limit when you can enroll for
coverage. For information, see  [MSU Benefits
Plus](/benefits/voluntary-benefits/index.html "Voluntary Benefits") .\
