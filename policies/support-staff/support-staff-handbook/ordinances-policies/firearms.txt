**Firearms Policy**

Except for peace officers authorized by the director of the Department
of Police and Public Safety, members of the University workforce are
prohibited at any time while on any property owned, leased, or otherwise
controlled by the University, or elsewhere in the course of their
employment with the University, from possessing or using any firearm.
This policy applies regardless of whether the member of the workforce
has a concealed weapon permit or is otherwise authorized by law to
possess, discharge, or use a firearm. For more information, please refer
to the [Firearms
Policy](https://hr.msu.edu/policies-procedures/university-wide/firearm_policy.html "Firearms Policy").
