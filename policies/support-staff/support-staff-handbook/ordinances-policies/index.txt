University Ordinances and Policies - Support Staff Employee Handbook {#university-ordinances-and-policies---support-staff-employee-handbook .page-title xmlns=""}
--------------------------------------------------------------------

This section highlights several University Ordinances and Policies. The
Board of Trustees of Michigan State University adopts ordinances that
provide for the care, preservation, and protection of the property
governed by the Board as well as for the security and well being of
persons on said property. Additional information including a complete
listing of ordinances are available online at the  [Board of Trustees
website](https://trustees.msu.edu/ordinances/index.html "Board of Trustees Website")
.
