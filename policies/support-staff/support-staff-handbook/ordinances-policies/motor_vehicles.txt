Support Staff

**Motor Vehicles**

Employees who drive and park their private vehicles on campus must
register those vehicles by paying an annual fee or through payroll
deduction. Operation of vehicles on campus is subject to the provisions
of the MSU Ordinances, State Motor Vehicle Code and the Employee Parking
Regulations. Copies of the regulations plus additional information are
available through the [MSU Police and Parking
Office](http://www.police.msu.edu) or by calling 517-355-8440.
:::
