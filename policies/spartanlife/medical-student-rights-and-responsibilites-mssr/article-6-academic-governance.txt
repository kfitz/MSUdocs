ARTICLE 6: ACADEMIC GOVERNANCE


6.1 Each medical college shall make provision for medical student
participation on appropriate governance committees within the college
and University as specified by the relevant bylaws.

6.1.1 This document shall be consistent with guidelines put forward by
the national professional organizations of the respective colleges.

6.1.2 Medical student representatives shall participate as voting
members on college committees relating to policy-making.

6.1.2.1 Each medical college shall inform its medical students in a
timely manner of the committee positions that medical students may hold,
their duties and lengths of appointment, and the process by which
medical students are selected for appointment.

6.1.3 At the University level, medical students (also referred to as
graduate-professional students) shall be selected and shall have voting
membership on the University Graduate Council, Academic Council, and
other committees as may be specified by the _Bylaws for Academic
Governance_.
