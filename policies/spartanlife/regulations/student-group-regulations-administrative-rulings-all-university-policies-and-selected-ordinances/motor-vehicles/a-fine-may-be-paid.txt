> A Fine May Be Paid:

[Click here to print this page]



A FINE MAY BE PAID:


1. Via the online payment system. Go to _www.police.msu.edu_ to access.
2. Via mail (check or money order payable to Michigan State University).
3. In the drop box in the MSU Police Department lobby (24 hours/7 days).
4. At the Parking Office during business hours.

If a fine is not paid within seven (7) days after issuance, late and
delinquent fees shall be added to the fine. In addition, failure to pay
violations could result in additional fees, a hold being placed with the
Registrar’s Office, a warrant issued for the registered owner’s arrest
(if the violation is transferred to 54-B District Court) and/or
impoundment of the vehicle at which time unpaid violations, the
impoundment fee and storage fees must be cleared to release the vehicle.
