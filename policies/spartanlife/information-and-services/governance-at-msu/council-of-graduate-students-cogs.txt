COUNCIL OF GRADUATE STUDENTS (COGS)


COGS is the all-University graduate and graduate-professional student
governing body. It is composed of one representative and an alternate
from each degree granting unit, nine officers, and the graduate student
representatives on University committees. COGS’ goals are to: promote
the academic, economic and social aims for all graduate students;
establish effective communication among these students and the
academic/administrative units of the University; and create channels of
effective communication with other student organizations. Located at 120
Chittenden Hall, (517) 353-9189, email: office@cogs.msu.edu. Website:
_www.cogs.msu.edu_.

SERVICES for graduate and graduate professional students include:

COGS COPY SERVICE provides self-service copying starting at 4¢ per page.
Service includes two-sided copying, color copying, enlargement/reduction
81/2 x 11, 81/2 x 14 and 11 x 17 copies, automatic document feeder and
collator. Faxes can also be sent. Cash or check only.

COGS LOAN PROGRAMS – Interest-free loans of $500 for 60 days are
administered through the Office of Financial Aid, 252 Student Services
Building. Interest-free loans of $300 for 8 weeks are administered
through the ASMSU Business Office, Student Services Building, Room 307.

LEGAL AID COGS provides for legal aid through Student Legal Services in
329 Student Services Building. They can be reached at (517) 353-3716 to
schedule an appointment.

EVENT FUNDING REQUESTS – Graduate groups and organizations are
encouraged to apply for funding. Applications and guidelines are
available online at _http://cogs.msu.edu/resources/funding/_.

CONFERENCE AWARDS – To help defray the costs of traveling to and from
conferences, COGS provides up to $300 in the form of a Conference Award.
Applications are available online at
_http://cogs.msu.edu/resources/funding/conference-award/_. Up to $300
can be awarded once during a graduate lifetime and funds are awarded by
lottery.

GSO SUPPORT FUNDING – If you have a departmental graduate student
organization (GSO) you can apply for up to $150 to help with meeting
costs when you meet to elect your COGS representative.

DISCOUNTED MEAL PLANS – COGS Discounted Meal Plans for Graduate and
Professional Students are available for purchase on the COGS website,
_www.cogs.msu.edu_.*

Use Meal access to bring friends, family and guests to eat with you at
the many dining options available. Enjoy meals like vegan, vegetarian,
kosher, halal, gluten-free, nutfree and more! You can even use it for
on-the-go dining for takeout in the dining halls 7:00 a.m.-midnights,
Monday –Friday.

*Must be purchased in bundles of 10, 5 bundles per grad student from
COGS per semester and must be used at least once during the fiscal year.
