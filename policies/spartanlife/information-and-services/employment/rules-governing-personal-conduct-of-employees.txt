RULES GOVERNING PERSONAL CONDUCT OF STUDENT EMPLOYEES


Rules of personal conduct for student employees are intended to promote
the orderly and efficient operation of the University, as well as to
protect the rights of all employees. The Rules Governing Personal
Conduct of Employees may be viewed at
_https://www.hr.msu.edu/policies-procedures/student-handbook/rules_governing.html._
 Students having questions or concerns regarding employee conduct should
contact the MSU HR Solutions Center at (517) 353-4434.
