COURTESY VAN AND PHARMACY DELIVERY SERVICES


The Courtesy Van provides transportation to patients who, because of
illness or injury, have difficulty getting to and from the Health
Center. Call (517) 353-4700.

The Pharmacy Delivery Service provides same or next day service
delivering both prescription and over-the-counter items. For details,
call the Pharmacy (517) 353-9165.

Both the Courtesy Van and Pharmacy Delivery services are available fall,
spring, and summer semesters, and are available to within one mile of
campus.
