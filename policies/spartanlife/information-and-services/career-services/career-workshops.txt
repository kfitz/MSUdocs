CAREER WORKSHOPS


WORKSHOPS are held frequently on a wide variety of topics. Check
_www.CareerNetwork.msu.edu_ and your _Handshake_ account for dates and
details. Workshop subjects usually include:

-   Resume Writing
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/resources-tools/resumes/index.html_)
-   Career Exposure events
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/exploring-options/career-exposure-events/index.html_)
-   Applying for Graduate School
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/resources-tools/applying-to-graduate-school.html_)
-   Job Search Strategies
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/jobs-internships/index.html_)
-   Internship Search Strategies
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/jobs-internships/internships.html_)
-   Interviewing
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/jobs-internships/interviewing/index.html_)
-   Career Fair Prep
    (_http://careernetwork.msu.edu/jobs-internships/finding-a-job/career-fairs.html_)
