CAREER ADVICE


CAREER ADVISORS can help at every stage of your planning for life after
college, whether your plans are employment or graduate study. MSU’s
Career Services Network is organized to meet the needs of students at
different stages or with specific interest areas.

-   Just getting started? CAREER SERVICES in ROOM 113 STUDENT SERVICES
    BUILDING (map: _http://maps.msu.edu/interactive/index.php?location=ssb_)
    specializes in the early stages of career exploration and planning
    such as clarifying interests and working toward choosing a major or
    career path. You can get help developing a resume, looking for
    part-time and Work Study jobs, and seeking internships.

-   Most colleges have a CAREER CONSULTANT who is a specialist in career
    options for majors in their college. They can provide detailed
    information about job search strategies for specific careers, as
    well as help you make connections with alumni from your academic
    college or employer representatives.

-   THE LEAR CORPORATION CAREER SERVICES
    CENTER (map: _http://maps.msu.edu/interactive/index.php?location=HB6M_)
    in the Broad College of Business provides career advising for all
    students interested in business-related careers. They also host
    interviews with hundreds of companies seeking interns or entry-level
    hires in business-related fields.

-   CAREER SERVICES @ THE
    STADIUM (map: _http://maps.msu.edu/interactive/index.php?location=HB6M_)
    offers employment resources for employers and is a primary location
    for hosting student interviews. They also manage the _Handshake_ job
    system and organize twelve major career events annually.

-   PHD/GRADUATE CAREER SERVICES (_www.careersuccess.msu.edu_) offers a
    full range of academic and non-academic professional development
    support for MSU graduate students.

INDIVIDUAL CAREER ADVISING To set up an individual advising appointment,
go to _www.CareerNetwork.msu.edu_ and click on the “Make an Appointment”
button. Business students should call the Lear Career Center at (517)
432-0830 to schedule an appointment.
