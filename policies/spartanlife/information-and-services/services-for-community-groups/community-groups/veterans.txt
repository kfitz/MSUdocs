VETERANS


Veterans, active service members, and spouses and dependents enrolled at
MSU who are seeking information for both on- or off-campus services are
encouraged to visit the Student Veterans Resource Center (SVRC) or call
(517) 884-5973 for more information. The SVRC is located in room 8 of
the Student Services Building. The SVRC offers a lounge area, computer
lab, and conference room for student veteran use. The SVRC also provides
information for the family members of service members and veterans.
Visit our website for more information, _veterans.msu.edu_ or email
veterans@msu.edu
