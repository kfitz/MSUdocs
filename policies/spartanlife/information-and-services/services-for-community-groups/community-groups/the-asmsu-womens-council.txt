THE WOMEN’S COUNCIL


WOMEN’S COUNCIL, an activity department within ASMSU, represents
women-identified students and sponsors programs throughout the year
focused on activism and working to create gender justice for all
undergraduate students. Women’s Council is a safe space to meet new
people and discuss women and gender equality related issues. Women’s
Council can be contacted by visiting;
_https://www.facebook.com/groups/212398802151690/_. Women’s Council also
serves on advisory boards for students, including the WOMEN’S ADVISORY
COMMITTEE TO THE VICE PRESIDENT FOR STUDENT AFFAIRS AND SERVICES.
