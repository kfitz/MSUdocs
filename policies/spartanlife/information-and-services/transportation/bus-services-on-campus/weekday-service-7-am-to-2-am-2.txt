WEEKDAY SERVICE 7 A.M. TO 2:30 A.M.


ACROSS CAMPUS ROUTES 30 & 31: travel from the east and west ends of
campus during the week. Buses run every 7 to 11 minutes until 7:00 a.m.
then every 15-30 minutes until 2:30 a.m. Route 30 travels between South
Neighborhood on the west and the East Neighborhood Boarding Area on the
east. Route 31 travels between the Brody Neighborhood on the west and
the East Neighborhood Boarding Area on the east.

COMMUTER LOT ROUTE 32: travels to and from the Commuter Lot #89 and the
MSU-CATA Transportation Center via Farm Lane weekdays. Runs every 11
minutes until 7 p.m., with service to the Clinical Center on all trips
departing the transportation center.

ROUTE 33: South and North Neighborhoods. Buses run every 7 to 10 minutes
until 7 p.m. when service concludes on this route.

UNIVERSITY VILLAGE & SPARTAN VILLAGE ROUTE 39: travels between the two
complexes via Harrison Road and onto campus to CATA’s Shaw & Farm
Boarding Center. Buses will operate every 13-20 minutes until 7 p.m.
then every 35 minutes until 2 a.m.
