WHAT, WHERE AND WHEN


Sources for information on events and activities include the following:

MSU CALENDAR WEBSITE: _www.events.msu.edu_
This calendar contains events from all across the University. It can be
sorted by date, type, location, etc.

DEPARTMENT OF STUDENT LIFE calendar: _studentlife.msu.edu._ Under “Quick
Links” click on “Calendar of Events”. This calendar contains Registered
Student Organization events.

MSU CABLE TELEVISION: Campus and local emergency notifications will be
displayed on every channel.  MSU Broadcasting Services offers select
classes and campus events on IVS-1 (22.1) and IVS-2 (22.2).  More
information about MSU’s Cable Television, including the channel lineup
can be found at _www.cabletv.msu.edu._
