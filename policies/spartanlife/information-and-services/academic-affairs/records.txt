ACADEMIC RECORDS


Office of the Registrar, 150 Administration Building, keeps your
academic records and transcripts. Additional services include:
certificates and diplomas; classroom and event scheduling; degree
certification; enrollment and registration; grade collection and
recording; fee classification; student athlete and veterans
certification; transfer credit processing; and university
curriculum. Telephone (517) 355-3300; fax (517)
353-1935; _www.reg.msu.edu._
