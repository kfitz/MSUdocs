COLLEGE OFFICES


Each college or major academic unit maintains an office or special staff
for assisting students with academic matters such as enrollment, changes
of major, class changes, academic advisement, and withdrawal and
readmission procedures. Not all of the offices are exactly alike, so
become familiar with the one appropriate for you.
