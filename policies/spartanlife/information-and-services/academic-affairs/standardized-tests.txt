MSU TESTING OFFICE


The Testing Office (located in 207 Student Services Building) is a
national test site for:

-   Computer-based tests such as GRE, MCAT, TOEFL, PRAXIS, MAT, and
    others
-   Proctoring for make-up exams and online courses through MSU or other
    institutions
-   Pearson VUE exams, including MTTC, GED, CompTIA, Microsoft, and many
    others
-   Employment certification exams through ISO-Quality Testing, Castle
    Worldwide, and Performance Assessment Network
-   CLEP and DSST exams for college credit
-   Paper-based exams such as ACT, SAT, LSAT, and GRE Subject tests
-   Proctored versions of the Math and Foreign Language Placement exams

The Testing Office is certified by the National College Testing
Association.  For more information, visit _www.testingoffice.msu.edu_ or
call (517) 355-8385.
