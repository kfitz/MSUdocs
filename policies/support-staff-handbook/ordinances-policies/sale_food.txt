**Public Sale of Food**

Food offered for public sale on the campus must, in general, be handled
only through Culinary Services, Division of Residential and Hospitality
Services. University organizations that wish to offer food service to
guests must obtain prior written approval from Culinary Services. For
details, contact Chandos McCoy with Culinary Services at 517-884-2525 or
the University Health Inspector, Betty Wernette-Babian, at 517-355-9700.
