Support Staff Policies & Procedures

Support Staff Policy & Procedure for Resignation / Voluntary Termination

Policy

Applies to: University support staff 
  • Nurses, APSA, APA employees are expected to give their supervisors notification of their intent to terminate at least one month in advance.
  • All other employees are expected to give supervisors at least two weeks notice.

Procedure

TERMINATION form: Department: Prior to the effective date of termination, the employing department completes the Termination form via the EBS Portal including:
  • last day worked,
  • reason for termination
  • letter of resignation

Termination pay:

  • Pay for hours worked will be included in the employee's final paycheck on the next scheduled pay date. Compensatory time and unused vacation accruals will be paid in a subsequent off-cycle pay. (VARIES: CTU employees remain on the payroll until vacation accruals are paid off.)
  • (NOTE: Discharged employees are paid on the last day of work or as soon as possible thereafter. See Policy and Procedure for Disciplinary Action.)

Benefits: 
MSU Human Resources: Sends a letter concerning rights and benefits of a terminating employee.

Employee:

  • Returns all University equipment, records, books, property, and settles all outstanding accounts, including:
    • accounts receivables,
    • student accounts receivables,
    • parking violation fines, and
    • library fines.
  • On last working day, employee is to:
    • return University keys,
    • return parking decal(s)
    • return University uniforms,
    • return ID card,
    • return credit cards
    • remove all personal items and
    • complete a Termination-Separation Checklist.

Refer questions to: MSU Solutions Center (telephone 517-353-4434 or e-mail  SolutionsCenter@hr.msu.edu )
