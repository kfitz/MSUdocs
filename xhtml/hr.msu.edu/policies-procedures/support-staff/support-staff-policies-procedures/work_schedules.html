Support Staff Policy for Work Schedules

Policy

Applies to: Regular and temporary University support staff. 

Purpose: Work schedules are established according to the needs and requirements of each unit and are designed to:

  1. Provide orderly and efficient service.
  2. Provide regularly consecutive hours of work where practicable.
  3. Avoid overtime.

The normal work week for most staff members consists of five 8-hour days, Monday through Friday. Office hours are normally from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., with one hour scheduled for lunch. A different schedule of hours and staggered shifts are maintained in some units.

Refer questions to: MSU Human Resources (telephone 517-353-4434, e-mail)
