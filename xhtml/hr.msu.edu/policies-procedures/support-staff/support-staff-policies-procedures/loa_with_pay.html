Support Staff Policy & Procedure for Leave of Absence with Pay

Policy

Applies to: University support staff

  • The University grants eligible employees leave of absence with pay in accordance with provisions as stated here and in individual policy statements and Collective Bargaining Agreements.
  • The types of leave of absence with pay are:
    • bereavement days,
    • holidays,
    • jury duty,
    • military duty (short-term),
    • personal leave days,
    • sickness and disability (including extended disability leave), and
    • vacations.

Procedure

Initiating leave with pay:

Employee:

  • Requests a leave of absence from immediate supervisor
  • Provides documentation as required by University policies and individual policy statement.
  • Pays benefit premiums to MSU Human Resources as appropriate    

Department:

  • Submits a Support Staff Request for Leave of Absence and sends form to MSU Human Resources stating whether position is to be held for the employee's return (NOTE: For certain leaves, positions must be held)
  • Reports leave in the EBS portal or through unity level absence

MSU Human Resources:

  • Upon receipt of the Support Staff Request for Leave of Absence and Return form, MSU Human Resources approves or disapproves the Leave of Absence
  • Completes leave of absence in SAP and notifies employee of leave approval and conditions for return to University employment
  • Bills employee for optional benefit premiums

Conditions of leave with pay:

  • While on leave of absence with pay, the employee retains all previously earned benefits, and will continue to accrue vacation and sickness and disability leave credits.
  • While on leave of absence with pay, the employee retains return rights to his/her position.
  • Compensation will be at the employee's base rate of pay.
  • University benefit contributions for health, dental, retirement, Long-Term Disability and Basic Life (for 180 days) plans will continue during leave of absence with pay. Other optional benefits may be continued with employee contributions.

NOTE: See Policy and Procedures for: bereavement days, holiday pay, jury duty pay, military duty pay (short-term), personal leave, sick leave, Long-Term Disability, Workers' Compensation, Extended Disability Leave (EDL), vacation pay and FMLA.
