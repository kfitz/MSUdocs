Archivist Handbook

3.2. Resignations

Archivists planning to resign should give at least sixty (60) days' notice in writing to the Director of the University Archives. Letters of resignation should include information regarding the last day to be worked as well as the termination date.

Archivists may contact the University's Benefits Office for information concerning the possible extension and conversion privileges of their Benefit Programs. Identification cards and keys should be turned over to the Director or Assistant Director.

The archivist will be offered an exit interview with the Director or Assistant Director.
