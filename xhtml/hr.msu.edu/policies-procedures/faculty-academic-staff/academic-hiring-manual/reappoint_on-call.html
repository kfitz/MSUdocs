5.3 Reappointing an On-Call Individual

Fill out a Reappointment/Rehire form. There are no attachments for U.S. citizens with the exception of the Form I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification if the employee was previously exempt from completing an I-9 or if there has been a break in paid service of one year or greater; send the completed Form I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification if the individual is a foreign national to Human Resources, Suite 110 Nisbet Building, East Lansing, MI 48823.

The Reappointment/Rehire form is routed in the same manner as described in Processing an On-Call Appointment.
