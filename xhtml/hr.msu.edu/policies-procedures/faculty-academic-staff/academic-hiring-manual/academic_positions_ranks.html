Academic Hiring Manual University Approved Academic Positions/Ranks

  • Also see University Approved Academic Titles.
  • "Emeritus" is added to the position/rank only for those granted this designation from MSU. Once emeritus status has been granted, any reappointments should show the position/rank emeritus as primary position/rank.
  • "Visiting" may be applied to any position/rank, when appropriate.  The visiting designation is only to be used for faculty and academic staff who are visiting on a short-term basis (i.e., a year or less) and who intend to return to their home institution or agency from which they are visiting.
  • "Clinical" is added in front of the position/rank of Faculty/Academic Staff appointed at no pay, whose duties involve patient care, or whose duties require a professional degree in a health or social services field. For example, Clinical Associate Professor.
  • "Adjunct" is added in front of the position/rank of Faculty/Academic Staff appointed on a no pay basis (except when "clinical" is appropriate). For example, Adjunct Assistant Professor, or Adjunct Visiting Research Associate.

Research associate

Research Associates are appointed on a fixed-term basis to work on research projects with MSU faculty; they are members of the academic staff of MSU.  Employment as a Research Associate is limited to a maximum of 60 full-time-equivalent service months.

Research associates must have a doctoral degree. (Ph.D., Ed.D., M.D., D.O., D.V.M.) or its equivalent.  Exceptions will be considered for the following:

1.  Individuals without a doctoral degree may be appointed with the rank of research associate for nine months provided the following condition is satisfied:

Satisfactory completion of the Ph.D. dissertation defense/orals with minimal revisions needed that are expected to be completed within three to six months.  A letter from the chairperson of the department/school in which the Ph.D. work is being taken indicating the nature of the work to be completed and an estimated time-frame for completion must be submitted with the appointment form.

Any reappointment beyond nine months requires proof of degree or a statement from the Dean of the Graduate School, or other appropriate University official, that the requirements have been completed.

2.  Individuals who have completed all requirements for the doctoral degree, but the degree has not been formally awarded, may be appointed for up to twelve months.  A letter from the Dean of the Graduate School, University Registrar's Office, or other appropriate University official must be submitted with the appointment form.

Sr research assoc

Senior Research Associates are appointed on a fixed-term basis to work on research projects with MSU faculty;  they are members of the academic staff of MSU.  The senior research associate is distinguished from the research associate by a substantial research publication record and scholarly recognition in the field and is expected to participate intellectually with faculty and graduate students.  A Ph.D. or its equivalent is required with experience to develop a substantial research and publication record.

May have any number of full-time-equivalent service months.

Appointment at this job title requires review and approval by Academic Human Resources.
