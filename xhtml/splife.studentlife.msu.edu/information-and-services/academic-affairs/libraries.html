Libraries

The MSU Libraries house a research collection of more than 5 million print volumes as well as several million e-books, over 50,000 print and e-journals, and hundreds of electronic databases. The library also provides a wide range of user services, including extended hours, one-on-one research assistance, printing services, group study rooms and the Assistive Technology Center. Special areas of interest to new students include the Careers Collection, Collaborative Technology Labs, Rovi Media Collection and the Gaming Labs, as well as the Film Series and other free events. The Main Library is located on West Circle Drive, across from Beaumont Tower.
