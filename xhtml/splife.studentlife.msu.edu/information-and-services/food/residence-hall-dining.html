Residence Hall Dining

No matter where you live, work, study or play, one of our 10 residential dining halls is nearby. MSU’s award-winning Culinary Services team provides our residents with culinary excellence from 7:00 a.m. to midnight, seven days a week in each Neighborhood. With made-to-order, healthy fresh dining in our residential dining halls including the recently renovated Heritage Commons at Landon, Brody Square, South Pointe at Case, The Vista at Shaw and the award-winning The Gallery at Snyder/Phillips, you are sure to eat well on campus no matter what you crave. Our flexible dining plans meet today’s students’ lifestyles, including take out options at all dining halls from 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., Monday through Friday.

Our professional dining staff is available to assist you in finding menu options that fit your needs, including vegetarian, vegan and allergy-friendly offerings. If you have dietary restrictions due to health concerns, food allergies or religious observances, you are encouraged to meet with and develop a relationship with our registered dietitian, executive chefs or dining hall managers to discuss options. Kosher cuisine is available at Wilson Dining Hall and Brody Square and halal foods are featured at Riverwalk Market at Owen Hall and Holmes Dining Hall. All of our residential dining hall menus will be marked with food labels for easy identification. For more information on menus and nutritional information, visit www.eatatstate.com. 

ON-CAMPUS STUDENT DINING PLANS
Dining plans for students living on campus support the lifestyles of today’s students, offering access to meals, snacks and drinks 7:00 a.m. to midnight, daily. (Residential dining plans are pre-arranged through student housing contracts.)

DineOn Platinum. Unlimited meals at any residential dining hall with $300 Spartan Cash and eight guest meal passes. Includes the use of one Combo-X-Change per day, Monday-Friday.

DineOn Gold. Unlimited meals at any residential dining hall with $150 Spartan Cash and four guest meal passes. Includes the use of one Combo-X-Change per day, Monday-Friday.

Dineon Silver. Unlimited meals at any residential dining hall. Includes the use of one Combo-X-Change per day, Monday-Friday.

OFF-CAMPUS STUDENT DINING PLANS
Dining plans for students living off campus and students living on campus in University Apartments, VanHoosen or Williams Hall. Tired of doing the dishes or living off of PB&J sandwiches? Choose from the following affordable meal plans:

  • DineOn 175 (175 meals), DineOn 125 (125 meals), DineOn 100 (100 meals), DineOn 50 (50 meals) and DineOn 25 (25 meals).
    • DineOn Campus – Use meal accesses at any of the residential dining halls.
    • DineOn-The-Go – Combo-X-Change (use meal access as a combo) at Sparty’s, Union Pizzeria, Serrano’s, Union Deli, Riverwalk Market at Owen Hall or the Eat at State ON-THE-GO food truck.
    • Use meal accesses to bring friends, family and guests to eat with you in the dining halls.
    • Off-campus dining plans don’t expire – you can use your meals as long as your MSU ID card is active! Plans are non-refundable.
  • DineOn Platinum, Gold and Silver dining plans are available to off-campus students.

Stay connected! Visit http://www.eatatstate.com, to gain access to dining hall menus, news and events, contact information and more. Feedback is welcome! If at any time you have a question or concern, call (517) 884-0660 or email info@eatatstate.com and you will hear back in a timely manner. In addition, like Eat at State on Facebook or follow @eatatstate on Twitter to hear about special events, giveaways, promotions and more!
