Public Eateries

Residential Dining Halls, (517) 884-0660, www.eatatstate.com. No matter where you live, work, study, or play, a residential dining hall is nearby. MSU’s award-winning Culinary Services team provides culinary excellence from 7:00 a.m. to midnight, seven days a week in each Neighborhood. Join us for a meal in our 9 residential dining halls including the recently renovated The Edge at Akers, Heritage Commons at Landon, Brody Square, South Pointe at Case, The Vista at Shaw and the award-winning The Gallery at Snyder/Phillips. With made-to-order, healthy fresh dining prepared by a dedicated staff led by 15 chefs, you are sure to eat well on campus no matter what you crave. In a hurry? Grab takeout from any dining hall with a Combo-X-Change swipe.

Sparty’s, (517) 355-4550, www.eatatstate.com/spartys. Visit us at one of our 21 Sparty’s Express, Refresh or Cafe coffee shops and convenience stores on campus. Sparty’s Express offers Spartan Spirit Coffee and convenience items for when you’re on the go. Sparty’s Refresh locations have Spartan Spirit Coffee, hot and cold specialty drinks and convenience items. Sparty’s Cafes offer made-to-order grill items or pizza, in addition to Spartan Spirit Coffee, specialty drinks and convenience. Combo-X-Change is accepted at all Sparty’s locations.

Sparty’s Market, (517) 355-4450, www.eatatstate.com/spartys. Sparty’s Market is an exciting, new urban, grocery shopping experience for the MSU campus community. Sparty’s Market will serve the new single family apartments adjacent to the market, the office staff within 1855 Place, and the students, faculty and staff of the MSU community and surrounding neighborhoods. Providing food that is sourced locally across Michigan as well as nationally, the market proudly delivers fresh selections and daily meal offerings from our chefs serviced hot from the grill or cold from the deli.

Eat at State ON-THE-GO Food Truck, (517) 355-8248, www.eatatstate.com/foodtruck. Conveniently parked on campus for lunch, visit the Eat at State ON-THE-GO food truck for portable, local and seasonal entrees, drinks and sides. Try the award-winning MSU’s own Smoked Cheddar Cheeseburger or other delicious entrees featuring local products. The food truck accepts cash, credit and debit cards, Spartan Cash, Eat at State Bucks and Combo-X-Change. Visit www.eatatstate.com/foodtruck for menus, locations and daily specials.

Crossroads Food Court in the International Center features Panda Express, Subway, Sparty’s Refresh, Woody’s Oasis, and UP Pizzeria at Crossroads accepting Combo-X-Change.

MSU Union offers Biggby Coffee, MSU Dairy Store, Sparty’s Express and the Union Food Court, featuring Union Pizzeria with hot, fresh and tasty pizzas, breadsticks, salads and more; Serrano’s southwestern fare; and Union Deli with soups, salads and sandwiches. All three Union Food Court locations accept Combo-X-Change. The MSU Union Food Court, featuring Serrano’s, Union Deli and Union Pizzeria, offers late night all-you-care-to-eat dining from 8:00 p.m. – midnight.

Starbucks, (517) 884-6727, is located at Wells Hall, Broad Business College, and 1855 Place as well as a Proudly Serving Starbucks locations at Broad Art Museum, offering freshly brewed coffees, handcrafted espresso beverages, Frappuccino blended beverages, Teavana teas and more. Bakery, warm breakfast and lunch items are available. Visit www.eatatstate.com/content/starbucks for specific location hours.

The State Room Restaurant and Lounge inside the Kellogg Center, adding a delicious edge to the Michigan State University Spartan experience, the State Room features some of the finest dining in the Greater Lansing Area. Our menus are changed seasonally to reflect the best local ingredients available, and our extensive wine list has earned the State Room Wine Spectator’s “Best Award of Excellence” award several years in a row. We also have several private dining rooms available, perfect for your next meeting or social gathering. Reservations: (517) 432-5049, kelloggcenter.com/stateroomrestaurant.
